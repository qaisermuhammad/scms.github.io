Scholarships at SCMS

SCMS believes that financial constraints should never be a barrier to quality education. We offer various scholarships to support deserving and talented students in their academic journey.

Types of Scholarships

We offer multiple scholarship programs to support our students.

Merit-Based Scholarship

Awarded to students with outstanding academic performance in FSc or previous degree programs.

Up to 100% Fee Waiver

Need-Based Scholarship

For students from underprivileged backgrounds who demonstrate financial need.

Up to 75% Fee Waiver

SCMS Excellence Award

For students who achieve top positions in their class during ongoing semesters.

50% Fee Waiver

Women in Healthcare

Special scholarship for female students pursuing careers in healthcare sciences.

25% Fee Waiver

Sports Scholarship

For students who have represented at district, provincial, or national level in sports.

25% Fee Waiver

Sibling Discount

Special fee concession for siblings studying simultaneously at SCMS.

15% Fee Waiver

Eligibility Criteria

Requirements to apply for scholarships at SCMS.

Academic Performance

Minimum 70% marks in FSc Pre-Medical or equivalent for merit-based scholarships.

Financial Need Documentation

Income certificate or affidavit for need-based scholarships.

Good Conduct

Must maintain good disciplinary record and attendance (minimum 85%).

Continuous Performance

For ongoing scholarships, students must maintain a minimum CGPA of 3.0.

No Other Scholarship

Students cannot avail multiple SCMS scholarships simultaneously.

Apply for Scholarship

Submit your scholarship application along with your admission form.
