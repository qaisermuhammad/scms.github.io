Who We Are

Safi College of Medical Sciences

Safi College of Medical Sciences (SCMS) is a premier institution of higher learning located in Mandani, Charsadda, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. Established with a vision to address the growing need for qualified healthcare professionals in Pakistan, SCMS offers comprehensive degree programs in nursing and allied health sciences.

Our college is proudly affiliated with Khyber Medical University (KMU) Peshawar, one of the most prestigious medical universities in Pakistan. All our programs are recognized by the Pakistan Nursing Council (PNC) and the Higher Education Commission (HEC), ensuring that our graduates receive degrees that are valued and accepted nationwide.

At SCMS, we believe in a holistic approach to education that combines rigorous academic training with extensive practical experience. Our modern campus features advanced laboratories, simulation centers, and a comprehensive library, while our partnerships with leading hospitals provide students with invaluable clinical exposure.

Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our commitment to excellence in healthcare education.

Our Mission

To provide quality education in nursing and allied health sciences that empowers students with the knowledge, skills, and values essential for delivering compassionate, evidence-based healthcare. We are committed to producing competent healthcare professionals who can meet the challenges of modern medicine and contribute positively to the health and well-being of individuals and communities across Pakistan.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in healthcare education in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and beyond, known for producing compassionate, competent, and innovative healthcare professionals. We envision SCMS as a leading institution that transforms aspiring students into skilled practitioners who excel as caregivers, leaders, researchers, and advocates for health in their communities and the nation.

Our Core Values

The principles that define our culture and guide every aspect of our institution.
