Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to secure your admission at SCMS.

1

Online Application

Fill out the online application form available on our website or collect a physical form from the college admissions office. Submit all required documents along with the application fee.

2

Document Verification

Our admissions committee will verify all submitted documents including academic certificates, CNIC, domicile, and other required paperwork.

3

Entry Test

Eligible candidates will be required to appear for an entry test conducted as per KMU policy. The test assesses knowledge in relevant science subjects.

4

Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be called for a personal interview to assess their motivation, communication skills, and suitability for the healthcare profession.

5

Final Selection & Enrollment

Final selection is based on merit (academic record + entry test + interview). Selected candidates must complete fee payment and enrollment formalities within the given deadline.
