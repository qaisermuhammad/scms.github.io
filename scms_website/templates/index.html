About Our College

Excellence in Healthcare Education Since 2026

Safi College of Medical Sciences, located in Mandani, Charsadda, KPK, is a leading institution dedicated to producing competent healthcare professionals. We offer comprehensive degree programs in Nursing, Medical Laboratory Technology, Radiology, Pharmacy, Physiotherapy, and other allied health sciences.

Our college is affiliated with Khyber Medical University (KMU) Peshawar and recognized by the Pakistan Nursing Council (PNC) and Higher Education Commission (HEC). With state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty, we ensure our students receive the best education and clinical training.
